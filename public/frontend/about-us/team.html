Management Board

Aminur Rashid

Chairman & CEO

Aminur Rashid finished his graduation under the prestigious IBA, The University Of Dhaka. He has 25 years of corporate and entrepreneurial experience and expertise under his belt. Having started his career in Siemens Bangladesh, he became the Director of Industry & Consumer Products Division of SIEMENS Bangladesh Ltd. within only 6 years.

After his success at SIEMENS, he had his heart set on a new venture. With the help of his team, he started the brand, SYMPHONY back in 2008.

Within just a few years it became the highest-selling cell phone brand in Bangladesh, holding around 45% of the total market share. This extraordinary series of achievements made him the great leader he is today. He started Edison Real Estate with the vision of building the most trusted real estate brand in Bangladesh.

Ahmed Pasha

Director
Ahmed Pasha finished both his Honors and Masters in Economics from the University of Dhaka and an additional MBA under the prestigious IBA (University of Dhaka).

Mr. Pasha has a diverse portfolio with over 20 years of professional experience. He has closely worked for Brand Development and Marketing activities for various local and international brands.

Subsequently, he has significantly contributed his knowledge and expertise with Nielsen Bangladesh, in addition to over 50 other research projects. After gaining adequate knowledge on consumer behavior, he joined SIEMENS Bangladesh to implement his learning where he led a business unit of OSRAM for 3 consecutive years.

Mr. Pasha has been a part of Edison Group since the very early days of the company. As a member of the top management board, he actively participated in developing a company strategy, policy, growth process and human development. Mr. Pasha has now taken up the challenge of making Edison Real Estate the most caring Real Estate brand in Bangladesh, as a board member.

Joyanta Prasad Roy

Assistant Director
Joyanta Prasad Roy, the Assistant Director - Head of Sales at Edison Real Estate Limited, brings over 18 years of extensive experience in the real estate industry, demonstrating his expertise in sales, marketing, and customer relations.

Since joining Edison Real Estate in May 2021, Joyanta has been instrumental in aligning sales strategies with business goals, conducting market surveys, and enhancing the sales team's capabilities through comprehensive training programs. Under his leadership, Edison Real Estate has seen a significant increase in sales and market presence, thanks to his strategic action plans and keen market insights.

His dedication to driving growth and achieving excellence makes him a valuable asset to Edison Real Estate Limited.
